## Style Prompt

Create a precise Chinese product promo for the Chrome extension "X 清道夫". The look is premium dark glass embedded inside an X/Twitter following page: deep blue-purple base, translucent panels, soft indigo highlights, careful red danger states, and restrained product motion. The viewer should feel that this is a focused local tool for pruning a noisy following list, not a social media growth product.

## Colors

- Base: `#0a0a14` for the deepest canvas.
- Surface: `#151525` for product panels and browser chrome.
- Glass: `rgba(18,18,32,0.72)` with blur for the extension toolbar.
- Accent: `#818cf8` to `#6366f1` for selected controls and focus.
- Danger: `#ef4444` to `#f43f5e` for unfollow and progress states.
- Success: `#10b981` for completion.
- Text primary: `rgba(255,255,255,0.92)`.
- Text secondary: `rgba(255,255,255,0.54)`.

## Typography

- UI and title: `-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "SF Pro Display", "Helvetica Neue", "Segoe UI", sans-serif`.
- Logs and counters: `"SF Mono", "JetBrains Mono", ui-monospace, monospace`.
- Use crisp Chinese interface labels with compact, product-like rhythm.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic SaaS gradients or bright blue X-brand dominance.
- Do not imply data scraping, cloud upload, or hidden analytics.
- Do not use fake real handles, real user photos, or real video IDs.
- Do not add playful mascots or decorative illustration unrelated to the browser workflow.
