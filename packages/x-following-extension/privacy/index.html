🔒 Privacy Policy · 隐私政策

X 清道夫 (X Scavenger) — Chrome Extension

Last updated: 2026-06-05

1. Data Collection · 数据收集

X 清道夫 does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

This extension operates entirely within your browser. It does not:

  • ❌ Collect your X/Twitter credentials or authentication tokens
  • ❌ Read, store, or transmit the contents of your X timeline, posts, or messages
  • ❌ Track your browsing history across other websites
  • ❌ Use cookies, localStorage, or any persistent storage
  • ❌ Send any data to external servers or third-party services
  • ❌ Include analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting SDKs

2. How the Extension Works · 扩展工作原理

All processing happens locally on your device:

  • The extension activates only on https://x.com/* and https://twitter.com/* following list pages (/following).
  • It reads the DOM (page structure) to identify user cells on the following list, and injects checkboxes and a toolbar UI.
  • When you choose to unfollow users, it clicks the "Unfollow" buttons on your behalf — the same actions you could perform manually.
  • No data leaves your browser. The list of selected users exists only in memory (JavaScript variable) and is cleared when you close or navigate away from the page.

3. Permissions Explained · 权限说明

Permission Why it's needed
scripting To inject the toolbar UI and checkboxes into the X/Twitter following page.
tabs To detect when you open a following page and automatically activate the extension. The extension does not read tab content from other sites.
host_permissions
(x.com, twitter.com) 		Required by Chrome Manifest V3 for content script injection on X/Twitter. The extension does not run on any other websites.

4. Third-Party Services · 第三方服务

This extension does NOT use any third-party services, APIs, or libraries that communicate over the network. The only network requests made are those inherent to your normal use of the X/Twitter website itself.

5. Children's Privacy · 儿童隐私

This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. It does not collect information from anyone.
